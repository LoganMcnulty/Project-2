Vocab-O-Matic

This app will send (via SMS or email) a list of vocabulary words to parents to use with their students. The problem is to expose students to new words outside of the classroom to help them develop a rich vocabulary. Users will enter contact information, preferences for topics, and the frequency that they want to receive the list of work. The app will send list of words based on these preferences.
